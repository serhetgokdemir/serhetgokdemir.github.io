Bank Marketing With Social and Economic Concept Through Data Analysis

In this project, I conducted an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Data Preparation (DP) on bank marketing data to uncover key patterns and relationships within the dataset. Through univariate and multivariate analyses, I visualized feature distributions and investigated correlations, preparing the data for further analysis. I then applied multiple dimensionality reduction techniques, such as PCA and t-SNE, to distill the data’s complexity while retaining essential information. This process offers insights into the data structure and provides a streamlined foundation for future predictive modeling and data-driven decision-making. The Github link will be added soon.
